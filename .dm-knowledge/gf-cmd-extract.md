- **[convention]** :Gitflow subcommands register from per-area modules under lua/gitflow/commands/, listed in AREA_MODULES in commands.lua. An area exports register(ctx) -- ctx.register(name, spec), ctx.config, ctx.commands -- and an optional ordered panels list that :Gitflow close walks. A spec is {description, run(cmd), category?, complete?}; commands.lua is wiring+dispatch only and never holds registrations.
